Visit Seattle Washington Today!

Green Lake

Kayak rentals are available at the lake, but you are also allowed and welcome to bring your personal kayak. The walking trail surrounding Green Lake is 2.8 miles long and wraps along the water on a paved and gravel path. The nearby park has basketball courts and plenty of trees for picnics or relaxing. Swimming is allowed while a lifeguard is on duty.

  • Duration: As long as you want
  • Price: $0-$50 USD
  • Safety: Little to no safety concerns, safe public park

Gasworks Park

Historic site of former gas plant which is now a 19.1 acre public park. Free entry with picnic tables and a beautiful view of Lake Union. Food and drink options are very limited at the park so bringing your own food and drinks or planning to eat nearby is advised.

  • Duration: Open Daily 6:00 am-10:00 pm
  • Price: Free
  • Safety: Little to no safety concerns, safe public park

Seattle Center

Various attractions such as the International Fountain, Space Needle, Museums, and much more. The Museum of Pop Culture is an excellent place to visit which is close to the Space Needle.

  • Duration: The Seattle Center is open 7:00 am-9:00 pm daily
  • Price: $0-$100 (Varies depending on visited attractions)
  • Safety: Very safe, security present in and around Seattle Center

Pike's Place

Extensive farmer's market with many offerings. Find everything from fresh fruit and vegetables to fresh fish caught that day, and various arts and crafts booths have something for everyone.

  • Duration: Pike's Place is open 9:00 am-5:00 pm daily
  • Price: $0-$100 (Varies depending on purchases)
  • Safety: Safe with security on site for vendors

Where can I stay?

There are plenty of hotels in the area that provide a wide range of accommodations that should be acceptable to everyone who plans to visit. Various campgrounds are also available for nature lovers who want to backpack and stay in a tent.

What can I eat there?

There are many restaurants in Seattle that offer many different types of food from a wide range of cultures. According to Curiocity Seattle is in the top 10 best locations for food in America. Seattle is filled with many restaurants, bars, coffee shops, bakeries, and more making it the perfect place for those of all palettes to visit.

How can I get to Seattle, Washington?

The best way to travel to Seattle, Washington, is to fly to Seattle-Tacoma International Airport. Tourists visiting from nearby states may also choose to drive directly to Seattle to avoid air travel.

Is it safe to visit Seattle, Washington?

Seattle is a safe place to visit. According to a local Seattle news station, Seattle is ranked the 158th safest city in America. Tourists should not have issues visiting the city as long as they practice common sense.
